From Student to Small Business Owner

Balancing computer science studies with a passion for sustainable fashion

How It All Began

VintageCycle started in 2023 as a side project while I was studying Computer Science at university. What began as a way to earn extra income by selling vintage items online quickly grew into a passion for sustainable fashion and small business ownership.

As a student, I noticed how much perfectly good clothing was being discarded while my peers kept buying new fast fashion. My mission became clear: give quality pre-loved items a second life while making vintage style accessible to students and young professionals on a budget.

Our Student Business Values

Sustainability

Every item we sell reduces fashion waste. We carefully clean and repair all items to extend their lifespan.

Student Focus

We understand student budgets and keep prices affordable while maintaining quality standards.

Tech-Enabled

My CS background helps run the business efficiently with smart digital solutions.

Quality

Every item is hand-selected for durability and style. If I wouldn't wear it, I won't sell it.
